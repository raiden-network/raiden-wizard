Terms of Use & important information for users

This Software, the Raiden Wizard, will assist you in setting up the requirements to join the Raiden Network in six steps.

Raiden needs
(i) access to the Ethereum blockchain,
(ii) a Raiden Account, and
(iii) three different tokens to be fully functional.

The Software will guide you through the process to fulfil the requirements.

Third Party Services

For you to be able to obtain the required tokens using the Software, the Software gives you the option to interact directly with certain web services provided by third parties, such as crypto-to-crypto exchanges - Kyber or Uniswap. The use of these options is purely voluntary and not required to use the Software if you fulfill the requirements.
